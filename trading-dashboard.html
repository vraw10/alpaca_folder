How to Fetch from Alpaca

GET /v1/trading/accounts/{account_id}/account

Returns the account object. Use the buying_power field directly.
Other useful fields: cash, cash_withdrawable, equity, portfolio_value

GET /v1/trading/accounts/{account_id}/orders?status=open

Returns all open orders. To calculate cash blocked:
For each buy order, sum (qty - filled_qty) × limit_price.
For market orders (no limit_price), use last trade price or mark price as an estimate.
