How to Fetch These Values

Buying Power

GET /v1/trading/accounts/{account_id}/account

Use the buying_power field from the account response. Other useful fields: cash, cash_withdrawable, equity, portfolio_value.

Cash Blocked in Open Orders

GET /v1/trading/accounts/{account_id}/orders?status=open

For each buy order, compute: (qty - filled_qty) × limit_price.
For market orders (no limit_price), use last trade price as estimate. Sum across all open buy orders to get total blocked cash.
